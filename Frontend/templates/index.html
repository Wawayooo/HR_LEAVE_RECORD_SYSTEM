About Osmeña Colleges

Established 1970 | Masbate City, Philippines

Osmeña Colleges stands as a beacon of educational excellence in Masbate City, having served the community for over five decades. Founded in 1970, the institution was established with a singular vision: to provide accessible, quality education that transforms lives and empowers individuals to achieve their fullest potential.

Our institution offers comprehensive programs in Business Administration, Teacher Education, Information Technology, and Liberal Arts. Each program is carefully designed to meet the evolving demands of the modern workforce while maintaining the timeless values of academic rigor and ethical leadership.

Through the years, Osmeña Colleges has produced thousands of graduates who have become successful professionals, entrepreneurs, educators, and public servants. Our alumni network extends across the Philippines and beyond, a testament to the quality education and character formation our students receive.

The LIFE philosophy—Leadership, Integrity, Fortitude, and Excellence—guides every aspect of our educational mission, ensuring that our graduates are not only academically competent but also morally upright and socially responsible citizens.
